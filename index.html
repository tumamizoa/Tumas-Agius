postform;

When the yarn begins to fray; when the storm can't help but break; when the tide is washed away...

...form
begins
to

stray.


A transdisciplinary examination into the acceptable limitations of contemporary form. The following thesis assumes that our continuous collapse into the inescapable expanse of entropy is embodied by a sense of collective discomfort, and proposes that an expressive structure applicable to a post-certainty world must abandon comfort in favour of a new wave of distructure that only becomes increasingly chaotic with the passage of time.
